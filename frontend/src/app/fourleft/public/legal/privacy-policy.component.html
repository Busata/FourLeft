Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fourleft.io ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services, including our Discord bot integration for Dirt Rally 2.0 and EA SPORTS WRC.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Discord user information when you interact with our bot
  • EA SPORTS WRC profile information you choose to share
  • Club memberships and racing statistics
  • User preferences and configuration settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Game statistics and leaderboard data from Dirt Rally 2.0 and EA SPORTS WRC
  • Usage data and interaction patterns with our services
  • Technical data including IP addresses, browser type, and device information

3. EA SPORTS WRC Data

When you use features related to EA SPORTS WRC, you acknowledge that:

  • EA SPORTS WRC requires an EA Account and acceptance of EA's User Agreement (terms.ea.com) and Privacy & Cookie Policy (privacy.ea.com)
  • EA may collect and store data about your gameplay, including statistics, system interactions, and peripheral hardware
  • EA uses this data to operate their services, improve products, provide updates, and communicate with you
  • You must be 13+ to use online features
  • Full EA policies are available at ea.com/legal

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Discord bot notifications and commands for club activities
  • Display racing results, standings, and statistics
  • Enable club configuration features (merging, filtering, tier divisions)
  • Post daily/weekly/monthly community results for opted-in users
  • Maintain and improve our services
  • Communicate with you about technical issues or updates

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • Within Discord servers where you've authorized our bot
  • Publicly on leaderboards and community results (if you've opted in)
  • With EA services when accessing EA SPORTS WRC data
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of community progress tracking
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13 years of age, consistent with EA's requirements. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

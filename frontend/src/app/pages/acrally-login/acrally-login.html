Assetto Corsa Rally

A companion tool for Assetto Corsa Rally that tracks and keeps a history of your stage results, so you can follow your progress. The final goal would be to offer some sort of club system where the tool guides you in what race to do and track the result.
Still in beta but feel free to test and let me know bugs/quirks. Source for the tool is available on github

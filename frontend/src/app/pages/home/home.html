Fourleft is an EA SPORTS WRC companion project, offering a Discord bot that:
  • Notifies channels when a new time is set
  • Offers commands to show results and standings
  • Tracks your EA SPORTS WRC profile
  • Awards rank badges

Contact @Busata#9432 on Discord for more information.

Found technical issues? Report them @Github issues

A bit of history

Fourleft started life as a Dirt Rally 2.0 project — a Discord bot for club notifications, results, standings and the daily/weekly/monthly community challenges. With Dirt Rally 2.0's online servers shutting down on the 8th of July 2026, that chapter has come to a close and Fourleft now focuses entirely on EA SPORTS WRC. Thanks to everyone who was part of the DR2.0 days!
